VegFood

Veg food consists of plant-based ingredients like vegetables, fruits, grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds. It excludes meat, poultry, and seafood, often focusing on health, sustainability, and ethical choices. Popular vegetarian dishes include salads, curries, stir-fries, and plant-based protein alternatives like tofu and lentils, offering diverse flavors and nutrition..

NonVegFood

Non-veg food includes meat, fish, eggs, and poultry, providing essential proteins, vitamins, and minerals. It is rich in iron, omega-3 fatty acids, and vitamin B12, supporting muscle growth and overall health. Popular dishes include chicken curry, grilled fish, and biryani. While nutritious, moderation is key for a balanced diet and a healthy lifestyle.

Juices

Juices are refreshing beverages made by extracting liquid from fruits, vegetables, or a combination of both. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, promoting overall health. Fresh juices provide hydration and natural sweetness without added sugars. Popular options include orange, apple, carrot, and green juices. Health benefits
